How to Play Block Breaker V1

Master the original Block Breaker with these essential controls and strategies:

Keyboard Controls:

  • Use Left Arrow key to move paddle left
  • Use Right Arrow key to move paddle right

Mouse Controls:

  • Move mouse to control paddle position
  • Click to activate power-ups

Touch Controls:

  • Swipe left or right to move paddle
  • Touch screen sides for movement
  • Double tap to activate power-ups

Block Breaker V1 Strategy Guide

Improve your gameplay with these essential tips:

  • Take advantage of paddle expansion power-ups for better control
